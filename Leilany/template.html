MARIA LEILANY





MARIA LEILANY ANGULO (1988)

The photographic work of Maria Leilany is the result of the assessment and interpretation around her. For her, photography has become her means of expression, passion and talent.
His photographic work has attended and participated in several exhibitions in Ensenada, Baja California, Mexico.
Also develops as a student of Communications at UABC, Mexico. And now as an exchange student in the area of Media Communications at Karlstad University, Sweden.
